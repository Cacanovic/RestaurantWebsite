THE SAVINJA VALLEY, SURROUNDED BY HOPS

Nature, environment and tradition express our story. We are returning to the basics that are neglected in everyday rush. An individual element of the environment and gratitude for every day is an inspiration that gives us strength.

Ambition has written a story of our three locations - objects with a cultural heritage. Former outbuilding of Novo Celje mansion, hotel in Novo Celje baroque park and Villa Å iroko are monuments of the past, which are seen through the eyes of the present. We take care for our heritage and upgrade it with our work. The prize is we can spend our working days in these mystical environments.

We create our tastes responsibly. Our development is our motivation. The team consists of individuals whose characteristics and values are the quality of our service. Friendship is what enriches our lives.
